Introduction

Jeiyien Trading Company Limited is a forward thinking, energetic and
dynamic Construction and General supplies Company.
Our philosophy is that all successful projects are founded on the people
responsible for the project and our team are dedicated to understanding
our client’s business objectives so that they can create the end results
which cater to their needs.
We are a growing company with a proven track record in delivering
Successful projects where all stakeholders would like to work
together again.
Our collaborative approach and commitment to
understanding, has enabled our client’s key objectives has ensured our
